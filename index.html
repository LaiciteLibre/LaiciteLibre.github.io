Laïcité Libre

Welcome to Laïcité Libre.

This website explains the key ideas of laïcité (secularism) and how it connects to liberation movements and secular thought in Iranian society.

Main Ideas

  • Laïcité means the separation of religion from government and public institutions.
  • Freedom of religion and freedom *from* religion must both be protected.
  • Iran's future requires dialogue on secular values, individual freedoms, and democratic governance.

About Us

This initiative supports public education and open dialogue about secular principles in Iran and beyond.
